Meet Our Team!

Uwaya Erdmann, CH, BSc, ND

Rooted in a background of traditional First Nation and European herbal medicine, Uwaya is a committed general practitioner of Naturopathic Medicine, licensed since 1999. She studied at Trent University (BSc), Bastyr University (Seattle), and CCNM (Toronto), and subsequently advanced homeopathy. She is a teacher and mentor and strives to provide comprehensive and effective naturopathic health care to people of all age groups and families. She is experienced in treating a wide range health conditions - acute and chronic.

Brenda Tapp, BSc, Doula, ND

Brenda grew up in Peterborough and attended Trent University here. After graduating with distinction and an Honours Bachelor of Science degree in biology, she went on to complete her Doctor of Naturopathic Medicine degree at the Canadian College of Naturopathic Medicine. Within the community Brenda volunteers as the team trainer of the Peterborough Varsity Wolverines football team. She has a family practice, but has a special place in her heart for perinatal health and cancer care. Brenda has additional training in reiki, IV therapy, cosmetic acupuncture, cancer care and is also a birth doula.

Heidi Willms B.A, ND

Heidi has a family practice helping patients of all ages develop a personalized and proactive approach to their health care as through Naturopathic Medicine. Heidi has a special interest in gastrointestinal health, women's health and endocrinology. Her approach is an integration of current evidence-based best practices in natural medicine, applied with an understanding of healing according to the traditional philosophy of naturopathic medicine. Heidi is a qualified Bowen Therapist. In addition to family practice, Heidi is employed as Clinic Faculty at the Canadian College of Naturopathic Medicine.

Chantal Langlouis, ND

Chantal is a Naturopathic doctor, certified Bowen therapist, and college professor. She holds a B.ScH from Queen's University in Life Sciences and an ND from the Canadian College of Naturopathic Medicine. Over the past 6 years Chantal has been focusing on a post-secondary teaching career in addition to practicing Bowen therapy. She greatly enjoys using Bowen therapy in order to help treat a variety of conditions most notably musculoskeletal conditions associated with pain.

Robin Griffin

Robin is our Office and Dispensary Manager; she has been with us since 2008. She has a background in retail and visual merchandising arts. This experience has afforded her with knowledge and skills that have transferred to the clinic. Robin has always looked to a more natural form of healing and is always eager to learn and share the information.
